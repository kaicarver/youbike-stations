ride.taipei - Youbike stations experimental site

中文

ride.taipei aims to provide real-time information on all Youbike stations in Taipei and New Taipei City.

Each station has its own web page with the following information, both in Chinese and English:

- the station name and number
- the area/address of this station
- numbers of available bikes and spaces at this station
- a map of this station and its neighborhood

Note: the information is currently not up-to-date. In a future version of this web site, the information will be updated every minute.
